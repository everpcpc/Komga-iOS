Privacy Policy

KMReader respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use the KMReader app.

Information Collection

KMReader does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or user data. All data related to your usage of the app, including authentication credentials, reading progress, and cached content, is stored locally on your device only.

Local Data Storage

To provide you with a better user experience, KMReader stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Server URL and authentication credentials
  • Cached images and content for offline access
  • App preferences and settings

All of this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties. We do not have access to any of this information.

Server Connection

KMReader connects directly to your Komga server instance. All communication occurs between your device and your server. We do not intercept, monitor, or store any data transmitted between your app and your server.

Third-Party Services

KMReader does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not include any code that would collect or transmit your personal information.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all information associated with KMReader by uninstalling the app. To ensure complete removal of authentication credentials stored in Keychain, you may need to log out from within the app before uninstalling.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the project's GitHub repository.
